All about My Skin Essentia

Tina started her career in the beauty industry in 2001, trained for a diploma in Beauty Therapy. Coupled with her caring nature and a strong desire to always do her best, she has developed an eye for detail in each of her treatments. This has helped her to instill from the start, the high expectations of a beauty salon environment in terms of client care and the high standard of treatments a beauty salon like My Skin Essentia has to offer.

Located within Lancaster hotel & Spa, within close proximity to Brunel University London. My Skin Essentia offers a wide range of advanced beauty treatments. There is a full menu of dermal fillers, laser treatments, waxing, and facials. The calm and friendly ambience makes it the ideal spot to unwind and enjoy the pampering you deserve.
